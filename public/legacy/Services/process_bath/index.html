BATH REMODELING

We provide following deliverables for your bath remodel project:

1. PLANNING OPTIONS

Based on your design preferences, we will create a design proposal which covers several arrangement options. We will discuss all proposed options, analyzing the pros and cons of each option. As a result, we will find the best layout that works for you and implements all needed details.

2. 3D PHOTOREALISTIC RENDERINGS

You will be able to easily imagine how the space will look with chosen materials and appliances all together. 

3. ACCURATE DRAWINGS

Drawings include layout plans, electric and plumbing plans with all necessary details, so you will get less questions from your contractor's team and everything will be in place as we planned.

4. ACCURATE ORDERS LIST

Orders list contains full information for all purchases (online or showrooms — you decide), so it's easy to track budget and delivery schedule.
